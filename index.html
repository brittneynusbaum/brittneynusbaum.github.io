About Me

My journey to becoming a software engineer began as anyone would expect;
by pursuing a degree in Musical Theater.

Just before the pandemic hit, I found myself yearning to jump back into an educational environment to jumpstart a new career. I explored avenues from HR certification to Paralegal work, but they lacked the interest I was looking for. I stumbled across a Bootcamp Prep course and was instantly hooked. I spent the next year and a half trying to self-educate and consider whether or not this was the right path for me. Is this just a hobby I want to pick up, or is this a long-term career I’m ready to join? By dipping my toes into a number of coding courses, I came to the (right) decision that I’ve found the best career.
