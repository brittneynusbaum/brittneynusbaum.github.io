About Me

My journey to becoming a software engineer began as anyone would expect;
by pursuing a degree in Musical Theater.

After college, I pursued acting by immersing myself in theatre and moving to New York City. Fast forward two years, I was not fulfilled in the way I hoped and no longer in the right headspace. Moving back to California I found myself yearning to jump into an educational environment to jumpstart a new career.

I explored avenues from HR certification to Paralegal work, but they didn't spark the excitement I sought. One day, I stumbled across a Bootcamp Prep course and was instantly hooked.

Over the next year and a half, I continued working in Customer Service while teaching myself to code. However, I had one question left to answer: is this was the right career path for me? By dipping my toes into a number of coding courses, I can confidently say I came to the right decision to start a career as a Software Developer!
